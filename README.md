# E-commerce Profitability Analysis by Product Category

## Project Overview
This exploratory data analysis (EDA) project investigates a dataset of 5,000 e-commerce transactions. The primary objective is to evaluate business performance by identifying which product categories generate the highest total profit, providing data-driven insights for strategic decision-making.

## Tools & Technologies
* **Language:** Python
* **Libraries:** Pandas (Data Manipulation), Matplotlib & Seaborn (Data Visualization)
* **Environment:** Google Colab

## Key Findings & Business Insights
Based on the data visualization of total profit across various product categories, the analysis reveals:
* **Top Performers:** The `Furniture`, `Home Decor`, and `Clothing` categories are the primary profit drivers for the business. Furniture significantly outperforms all other categories, generating the highest total revenue.
* **Underperformers:** Conversely, `Sports`, `Beauty`, and `Groceries` contribute the least to the company's overall profit margin.
* **Strategic Recommendation:** The business should prioritize marketing budgets and inventory expansion for the top-performing categories (Furniture and Home Decor). Additionally, management should conduct a price-margin review on the underperforming categories (like Groceries) to determine if they are worth keeping in the product catalog or if operational costs are eating into their profits.
